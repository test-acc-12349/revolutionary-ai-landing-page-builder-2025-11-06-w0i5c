About AIBuilder

Founded in 2023 by a team of AI researchers, designers, and entrepreneurs, AIBuilder emerged from a simple observation: creating professional landing pages shouldn't require months of learning or expensive freelancers. We set out to democratize web design by combining cutting-edge artificial intelligence with an intuitive user interface. Our founding team spent years working at leading tech companies like Google, Adobe, and Figma, where they developed deep expertise in design systems, machine learning, and user experience. Today, AIBuilder has grown into a trusted platform serving thousands of creators, startups, and enterprises worldwide, powering over 50,000 landing pages that generate millions in revenue for our users.

Our mission is to empower everyone to create beautiful, high-converting landing pages without barriers to entry. We believe that great design and technology should be accessible to all, not just those with deep pockets or technical expertise. We're committed to continuously innovating our AI algorithms, expanding our template library, and building features that solve real problems for our users. Our core values – accessibility, quality, speed, and customer success – guide every decision we make. We invest heavily in research and development, ensuring our platform stays at the forefront of AI-powered design technology. We're not just building a tool; we're building a community of creators who are changing the world through their ideas and innovations.
